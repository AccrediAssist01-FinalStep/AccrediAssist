PROBLEM ANALYSIS & EXISTING SYSTEM STUDY

Project: AccrediAssist

Version: 2.0

Introduction

Educational institutions generate a huge amount of academic information every day. Faculty members, students, Training & Placement Officers, and coordinators share updates through official WhatsApp groups regarding workshops, industrial visits, internships, placements, research papers, certifications, cultural activities, sports events, seminars, guest lectures, and faculty achievements.

While WhatsApp has become the primary communication platform, it is not designed as a long-term institutional knowledge repository. As conversations grow, important information becomes difficult to locate and reuse.

Consequently, departments spend significant time manually searching old chats and preparing reports during NBA, NAAC, and AICTE accreditation.

Existing System

Currently, most institutions manage accreditation evidence using a combination of:

WhatsApp Groups
Microsoft Word
Microsoft Excel
Google Drive
Email
Physical Documents

When accreditation begins, faculty manually collect information from these sources.

Faculty/Students

↓

Share updates in WhatsApp

↓

↓

↓

↓

↓

Submitted for Accreditation

This process is repetitive, slow, and error-prone.

Problems in the Existing System
1. Information Loss

As WhatsApp conversations increase, earlier messages become difficult to retrieve.

Examples:

Workshop details
Industrial visits
Student achievements
Faculty achievements
Internship updates
Placement information
2. Manual Documentation

Faculty members manually:

Search old chats.
Copy information into Word documents.
Organize photos.
Prepare reports.
Verify dates and participant names.

This consumes significant time.

3. No Centralized Repository

Information is distributed across multiple platforms.

For example:

WhatsApp
Google Drive
Personal laptops
Excel sheets
Email

There is no single source of truth.

4. Difficult Accreditation Preparation

NBA, NAAC, and AICTE require evidence such as:

Workshop reports
Faculty publications
Student achievements
Placement statistics
Internship records
Industrial visits

Collecting this evidence manually delays the accreditation process.

5. Poor Search Capability

Searching for information like:

"Show all internships completed in 2025."

or

"Find sports achievements."

is nearly impossible using WhatsApp alone.

6. Report Generation is Time-Consuming

Departments prepare:

Monthly reports
Workshop reports
Industrial visit reports
Placement reports

These are created manually using Word.

7. No Automatic Classification

Messages are not categorized.

For example:

Sports
Cultural
Technical
Placement
Internship
Research

Everything remains as plain chat messages.

Need for the Proposed System

A modern accreditation support platform should:

Automatically collect institutional information.
Understand message content.
Categorize records intelligently.
Store them permanently.
Enable intelligent search.
Generate reports automatically.

This is the motivation behind AccrediAssist.

Scope of the Project

The project focuses on:

WhatsApp-based information extraction.
AI-powered classification.
Institutional knowledge management.
Smart search.
Automated report generation.
Placement and internship tracking.
Accreditation evidence management.

The project does not aim to replace a full College ERP.

Expected Outcomes

After implementation:

Faculty workload will be reduced.
Reports can be generated faster.
Institutional records become searchable.
Historical information is preserved.
Accreditation preparation becomes more efficient.
Cursor Notes

Cursor should understand that the core objective is not chat management.

The primary goal is:

Transform unstructured WhatsApp conversations into structured institutional knowledge that supports accreditation and reporting.

This objective should guide every design and implementation decision.
